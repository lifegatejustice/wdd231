Movie Website - Site Plan

Site Name

LaineMania

This name was chosen because it reflects the passion and enthusiasm for movies that the website aims to foster. The term "Mania" suggests an intense interest or obsession, which aligns well with the target audience of movie enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

LaineMania serves as a comprehensive hub for movie lovers, offering:

  • A curated collection of at least 15 movie entries.
  • Multiple Pages with Proper Metadata
  • Responsive Design & Standards Compliance
  • Latest movies
  • Latest news and updates from the film industry

Scenarios

  1. What are the top-rated movies in the thriller genre released in the last year?
  2. How can I keep track of movies I want to watch in the future?
  3. Where can I find honest, user-generated reviews for the latest blockbuster release?
  4. What movies are similar to my favorite film, and why are they recommended?
